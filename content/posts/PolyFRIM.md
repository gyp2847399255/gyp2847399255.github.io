# One-to-Many Multivariate Polynomial Commitment from Fast Reed-Solomon IOPs and Its Application to Asynchronous Verifiable Secret Sharing

## Introduction and Motivation

A \emph{polynomial commitment scheme} (PCS) enables a prover to commit to a polynomial $f$ defined over a field $\mathbb{F}$ with degree bound $d$ and variable number $\mu$. When given a point $\vec{x} \in \mathbb{F}^{\mu}$, the prover can convince a verifier via an evaluation proof that the committed polynomial $f$ satisfies $f(\vec{x}) = y$ for a public $y \in \mathbb{F}$.
